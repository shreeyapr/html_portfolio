Top movies of All times according to Shreeya

My 3 favourite movies.

3 Idiots

3 Idiots is a Bollywood comedy-drama that has a number of positive messages about friendship, but also features themes around suicide and problematic humor. It follows three friends, Rancho (Aamir Khan), Farhan (R. Madhavan), and Raju (Sharman Joshi), during their college years and beyond.

Yeh Jawani hai Dewani

Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani is the story of the relationship between two characters, Bunny & Naina, at two separate but defining times in their lives.

Vivaah

Romantic drama and musical film about the story of Poonam and Prem, a couple whose love and faith are tested by an accident
